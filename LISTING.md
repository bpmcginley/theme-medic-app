## Detailed description

Every Shopify store collects baggage. You try an app, uninstall it — but the
code it added to your theme stays, loading on every page, forever. Add a few
years of trying apps and your store is carrying dozens of dead scripts that slow
it down and quietly cost you sales (every extra second of load time can cut
conversions by ~7%).

**Theme Medic finds it and shows you exactly what to do.**

- **Deep theme scan.** We read your live theme and match it against a database of
  60+ common apps (Loox, Klaviyo, Judge.me, Yotpo, Bold, Vitals, and many more) to
  find the code each one left behind.
- **Ghost vs. active.** We check what actually loads on your live storefront, so
  we can tell the difference between an app that’s still running and dead code from
  one you uninstalled months ago. We never tell you to remove something that’s live.
- **Real performance cost.** Each finding shows its file weight, request count, and
  blocking time — so you know what cleaning it up actually buys you.
- **Daily monitoring (Pro).** Theme Medic re-scans every day and emails you the
  instant a new app leaves junk behind. Your store stops getting slower behind
  your back.

Theme Medic is **read-only** — it inspects your theme and storefront, and never
edits your theme without you. Setup takes one click: install, scan, done.

**Free:** 3 deep scans per month.
**Pro ($19/mo, 7-day free trial):** unlimited scans + daily monitoring & alerts.
